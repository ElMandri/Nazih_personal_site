About Me

I grew up in Morocco where I did a bachelor-level curriculum in psychology and social sciences followed by a master's degree in social psychology in France. My scientific interest is in the collective social thinking about the future and decision-making in the context of environmental and societal risks. My PhD project is on the collective social thinking and decision-making about the future in the context of drought in France and Morocco.
